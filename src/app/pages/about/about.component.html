Who are we and what is our story?

We are a manufacturing facility based in Bulgaria that specialises in producing 3D printed products. We make our rollers affordable without compromising their durability because what we value most is the quality of the product and the satisfaction of the user. 

 

Our team 

We are a group of engineering students and together with professionals in the beekeeping area we worked hard and extensively to create a honeycomb roller that will be of use for small and large beekeepers. 

 

Our mission and our values 

The goal we had set for ourself to achieve was to give an alternative to beekeepers, that is both effective and affordable. We also made sure that the materials used in production are 100% harmless to the bees (officially certified). What we value most is quality and that’s why our rollers are made with the best possible polycarbonates found on the market.
